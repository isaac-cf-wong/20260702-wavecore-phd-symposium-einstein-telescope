## The takeaway

<p class="large-claim">The Einstein Telescope is a new observatory, but also a new class of sensing and inference system.</p>

<ul>
  <li>Its science depends on measuring incredibly weak waves with controlled systematics.</li>
  <li>Its engineering depends on environmental sensing, calibration, synchronization, and control.</li>
  <li>Its discoveries depend on scalable, trustworthy inference from non-ideal data.</li>
</ul>

<p class="takeaway">For a waves, communications, sensing, and signal-processing community, ET is not adjacent; it is central.</p>
